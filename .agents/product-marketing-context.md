# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-05-13*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:**
一个住在桌面上的 AI 小伙伴。

**What it does:**
Desktop AI Companion is a desktop-resident AI companion that stays on the user's screen, reacts with visible character states, supports lightweight chat, and remembers a small amount of personal context like nickname and preferences. It is designed to feel present and calming, not like another browser chat tab.

**Product category:**
桌面 AI 伙伴 / AI 桌宠 / 陪伴型桌面助手

**Product type:**
Desktop software

**Business model:**
Early alpha / beta stage. Likely future freemium with paid upgrades for stronger models, richer memory, more expressions, voice, and premium characters.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:**
Not B2B-first. Primary audience is individual desktop users.

**Decision-makers:**
- Individual users
- Early adopter hobbyists
- AI power users

**Primary use case:**
Keep a lightweight AI companion on the desktop that can chat, react, and provide a small sense of presence during work, study, or creation.

**Jobs to be done:**
- Give me a calm AI presence while I work or study.
- Let me ask quick questions without opening a separate chat tool.
- Make the desktop feel less empty and more alive.

**Use cases:**
- Working alone for long stretches
- Writing, studying, coding, or designing
- Light emotional companionship without heavy commitment
- Quick AI check-ins during focus sessions

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:**
Most AI tools are utility-first and tab-first. They answer questions, but they do not feel present on the desktop.

**Why alternatives fall short:**
- 普通聊天机器人像工具，不像陪伴
- 桌宠通常只有视觉，没有智能对话
- AI 网页产品要主动打开，不能自然常驻

**What it costs them:**
- 工具切换频繁
- 桌面体验单调
- 独自工作时缺少轻量存在感

**Emotional tension:**
Users want something that feels alive and responsive, but they do not want something noisy, invasive, or creepy.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** AI companion apps and desktop pets with chat layers — often fall short because they are either shallow on companionship or generic on AI.

**Secondary:** ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot-style chat tools — solve questions, but not desktop presence or companion feeling.

**Indirect:** Traditional desktop pets, widgets, or music/video background companions — can soften loneliness but cannot respond intelligently.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- 桌面常驻
- 角色有可感知状态变化
- 轻量记忆
- 轻陪伴 + 轻助手双重价值

**How we do it differently:**
We combine a desktop-resident character, visible state transitions, and lightweight AI chat into one single ambient presence.

**Why that's better:**
Users feel less like they are opening a tool and more like they are interacting with something already there.

**Why customers choose us:**
Because this is not just another AI chat box. It has presence.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| 这不就是套了 Live2D 的聊天机器人吗？ | 差异不在模型接入，而在桌面常驻、可感知状态、轻记忆和不打扰的陪伴体验。 |
| 会不会很打扰工作？ | 产品核心原则之一就是“需要时在，专注时安静”，并支持免打扰和低存在感待机。 |
| 会不会记太多让我不舒服？ | P0 只做轻量记忆，且应让用户知道记住了什么，并能查看和删除。 |

**Anti-persona:**
Users who only want maximum-efficiency AI automation and do not care at all about character presence or desktop companionship.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:**
普通 AI 工具太像冷冰冰的工具，桌宠又太笨。

**Pull:**
她在桌面上，有反应，有一点记忆，感觉更像陪伴。

**Habit:**
Users are already used to browser AI and may default back to opening a tab.

**Anxiety:**
Users may worry that the product is gimmicky, distracting, or emotionally weird.

**Glossary:**
| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|

## Goals
**Business goal:**
Validate that a small cohort of users will keep a desktop AI companion open and come back voluntarily.

**Conversion action:**
Apply for alpha / install and use repeatedly.

**Current metrics:**
Not yet formalized. P0 should track install success, launch stability, chat success, history restore success, and multi-day voluntary reuse.

**Current alpha objective:**
Recruit 20-50 seed users who care about character presence, desktop companionship, or low-friction AI help, then learn whether they come back because she feels present rather than merely functional.
